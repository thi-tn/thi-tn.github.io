T.A.G. Labs Website

Overview

T.A.G. (Technology and Gaming) Labs is a D.C. based nonprofit launched in 2013 by technology professionals with a passion for teaching young people coding and other S.T.E.A.M. related skills through the building of interactive gaming experiences.

My Role

I started being an intern at T.A.G. Labs in March 2017. Together with the Web Design/Development team, I worked on the T.A.G. Labs website. My usual tasks included adding new content to the website, which ranged across various forms such as interactive graphics and webpages.

As an intern, I assisted the team with researching, designing visual mockups, developing website components and implementing them onto the Wordpress platform.

The Design

While working on the website, I followed two main objectives:

  • To make the T.A.G. Labs website engaging to students and potential sponsors.

  • To keep the styles of the newly added content consistent with the original web theme in particular and with the T.A.G. Labs brand identity in general.
