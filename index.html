My name is Thi Nguyen; I'm a multidisciplinary designer from Vietnam and currently based in Washington, DC. As a big art and tech enthusiast, I love bridging visual arts with computer technology. Believing in the power of design to have significant social impact, I aspire to design meaningful and inclusive experiences for all people, especially the underserved. With an ambistious vision and a strong passion, I am pursuing a career in UI/UX design and interactive art.

While I'm not working or learning, I'll most likely be brewing coffee, surfing Soundcloud, doodling, or taking a photo walk.
